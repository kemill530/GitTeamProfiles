Mike's Profile

BORN INTO a science fiction family, Mike read about science fiction easter eggs from an early age, imagining their existence as being from ulterior realities. This became a serious game as he grew up and learned the actual mathematics behind such thoughts. Thus his studies of astrophysics commenced. This of course is a fictionalized account written as a profile for Team Venice.
